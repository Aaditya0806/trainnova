Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to offer a permanent job after the internship?

No. You may choose to absorb only the interns who prove themselves. There is no obligation to offer permanent employment.

Who pays the stipend?

You do — directly to the intern. This replaces salary and comes without statutory obligations like PF or ESI.

Is this model legally compliant?

Yes. TrainNova works with TISS SSE under a Work Integrated Training model aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP), and interns are students — not contractual employees.

Can this be counted toward our CSR initiatives?

Absolutely. TrainNova supports your CSR skilling mandates with measurable outcomes and academic credibility .
