About Us

The TrainNova Triad - Learning That Works

At the heart of TrainNova is a 3-way partnership that makes the impossible possible Pillar and Role:

TISS SSE

Provides the academic framework, curriculum, certification & credibility

Employer

Offers real-world, On-the-Job Training (OJT) + monthly stipend.

TrainNova

Orchestrates execution — mobilization, onboarding, classes, exams & more.

Together, this system delivers a Work Integrated Training experience that is NEP-aligned, industry-relevant, and student-centric

Student Lifecycle: Step-by-Step

1. Mobilize

We identify 10+2 pass youth — especially from rural or underserved communities — and introduce them to the TrainNova pathway through schools, NGOs, social workers, and village influencers.

2. Onboard

Students are onboarded into a partner company as paid interns. At the same time, they are enrolled in an academic program offered by TISS School of Skill Education.

3. Earn + Learn

Each week follows a structured cycle:

5 days On-the-Job Training (OJT) at the workplace

1 day of classroom instruction (theory, life skills, digital skills)

1 day rest

Students earn ₹15,000 to ₹20,000/month — while progressing toward a certified degree.

4. Support Throughout

TrainNova manages attendance, exam prep, performance tracking, mentorship, and issue resolution — so that students stay on track, employers get reliable performance, and TISS ensures academic outcomes.

5. Exit with Credentials

The program runs for 3 years — but students can exit after Year 1 (with Certificate) or Year 2 (with Diploma), as per the National Education Policy (NEP). Those who complete all 3 years receive a full Bachelor’s Degree from TISS SSE.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the schedule like in TrainNova?

Each week includes 5 days of work, 1 day of classroom learning, and 1 day of rest. Students earn a stipend while working toward a degree.

Can I exit after one or two years?

Yes. You can exit after Year 1 with a Certificate, Year 2 with a Diploma, or complete all 3 years for a Bachelor’s Degree — all awarded by TISS SSE.

Who manages the classes and exams?

TrainNova coordinates faculty, scheduling, attendance, and exam logistics — while TISS provides the academic framework and issues the final certification.
