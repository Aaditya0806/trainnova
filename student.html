Frequently Asked Questions

Will my child get a job after this?

Yes — and during it too. They earn ₹15,000–₹20,000/month while learning, and get hired full-time by many employers after graduation.

Is this safe for girls?

Absolutely. We work with trusted companies and assign mentors. Many girls thrive and become the pride of their villages.

Is this a government scheme?

No — it’s better. It’s a program backed by TISS SSE, fully aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP).

What does it cost us?

Nothing. The company pays the student’s stipend and their tuition. You pay ₹0.

Can my child leave after one year?

Yes. After Year 1 they get a Certificate, after Year 2 a Diploma, and after Year 3 a Degree — all from TISS SSE.
